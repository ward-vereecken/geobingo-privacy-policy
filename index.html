GeoBingo Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2025

GeoBingo helps you explore the world through engaging geography challenges. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our services, including the GeoBingo mobile and web apps (collectively, the “Services”).

Information we collect

You may use GeoBingo without creating an account. If you choose to create an account, we may collect the following information:

  • Name (from Google or Facebook login)
  • Email address
  • Authentication identifiers
  • App progress, rewards, and usage data

How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save progress, streaks, and rewards
  • Provide core app functionality

We do not sell personal data.

Third-party services

GeoBingo uses third-party authentication providers:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Facebook Login

These services process your data according to their own privacy policies.

Cookies, local storage, and analytics

We use local storage and similar technologies to remember preferences, maintain session security, and improve app performance.

Advertising and monetization

GeoBingo may display advertisements in the mobile app to support development and keep the app free to use.

We use third-party advertising providers (such as Google AdMob) to serve ads. These providers may automatically collect and process certain information for purposes such as delivering ads, measuring performance, preventing fraud, and improving ad relevance.

This may include:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • IP address (used to estimate general location)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA if permitted)
  • App usage data (such as ad impressions, clicks, and interaction)
  • Diagnostics and crash data

Depending on your region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we may request your consent before showing personalized ads.

You can change your advertising consent at any time by adjusting your device privacy settings, resetting your advertising ID, or using in-app consent options if available.

We do not sell your personal data. However, advertising partners may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data retention

Account data is retained until you request deletion. Backup data may be retained for up to 30 days for security and legal purposes.

Children’s privacy

GeoBingo is not directed at children under the age of 9. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. No system is completely secure, so we encourage strong passwords and responsible account use.

Legal basis for processing

We process personal data only when necessary to provide the Services, fulfill a user request (such as account creation), comply with legal obligations, or with your consent where required.

International data transfers

Some of our service providers may process data on servers located outside your country. We take reasonable steps to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable laws.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, restrict or object to certain processing, and request a copy of your data.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

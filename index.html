Privacy Policy

GeoBingo Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

GeoBingo helps you explore the world through engaging geography challenges. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our services, including the GeoBingo mobile and web apps, accounts, saved progress, email communications, and any related offerings (collectively, the "Services").

Information we collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly and information that is generated when you use the Services.

  • Account details such as your username, display name, email address, and password (stored in encrypted form).
  • Gameplay data, including lesson progress, streaks, leaderboards, and earned rewards.
  • Saved preferences like language, theme, sound selections, and profile customization choices stored on your device or in the cloud.
  • Support requests and content you share with us when you contact customer care or participate in community features.
  • Approximate device and usage data such as app version, device type, and interaction events that help us improve app stability and performance.
  • Advertising identifiers such as your mobile advertising ID (AAID/IDFA) and related device identifiers used for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Ad interaction data such as ad impressions, clicks, and reward events.

How we use information

We use your information to deliver delightful geography-learning experiences and to operate essential business functions.

  • Create and manage your GeoBingo account, sync your progress across devices, and authenticate your access.
  • Provide personalized lessons, quizzes, and recommendations based on your saved progress and preferences.
  • Send important service messages, password resets, or account alerts.
  • Serve and measure ads, including limiting ad frequency and preventing abuse.
  • Monitor performance, prevent fraud or abuse, and improve the stability and accessibility of the Services.

When we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers that support core operations such as authentication, cloud hosting, analytics, customer support, and email delivery.
  • With advertising partners and ad networks (for example, Google AdMob) to serve and measure ads.
  • To comply with legal obligations, protect rights and safety, or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

Data memory

We retain your account and gameplay information for as long as your GeoBingo account is active. If you delete your account or request erasure, we will remove or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period unless we need to keep it for legal, regulatory, or security reasons.

Your choices and controls

You can access, update, or delete many aspects of your data directly in the Account screen. Additional options include:

  • Updating your email address, display name, and password from the Account screen.
  • Adjusting notification and email preferences or opting out of marketing communications via the links provided in our emails.
  • Controlling ad personalization and tracking in your device settings (iOS App Tracking Transparency, Android Ads settings).
  • Requesting account deletion, data export, or privacy assistance by contacting us at ward.vereecken@hotmail.be.

Children's privacy

GeoBingo is designed for a general audience and is not intended for children under the age where parental consent is required in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

International data transfers

We may process and store information on servers located in different countries. We maintain safeguards to ensure that international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your information remains protected regardless of where it is processed.

Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data, including encryption in transit, restricted access controls, and ongoing monitoring. No system is completely secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your login information confidential.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features, legal requirements, or feedback. We will post the revised policy in the app and update the "Last updated" date. Significant changes may also be communicated via in-app messages or email.

Contact us

If you have questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or GeoBingo's data practices, please contact us at ward.vereecken@hotmail.be. We aim to respond promptly and resolve issues in line with applicable data protection regulations.
